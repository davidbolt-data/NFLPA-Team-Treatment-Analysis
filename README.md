# NFLPA Team Treatment Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes NFL Players Association (NFLPA) report card data to evaluate how teams treat players across categories such as facilities, nutrition programs, travel accommodations, and family support. The goal is to identify which organizations provide the best player environments and what factors most influence overall team satisfaction.

## Business Question
Which NFL teams score highest in player satisfaction, and what organizational factors contribute most to positive player experiences?

## Dataset
NFLPA Team Report Card data containing grades for multiple organizational categories such as:

- Facilities
- Nutrition programs
- Travel accommodations
- Family support
- Coaching staff

## Tools Used
- Python  
- Pandas  
- Tableau  
- Excel  

## Analytical Approach
Letter grades were converted to numeric scores to allow quantitative comparison across teams. Average scores were calculated to create an overall team ranking. Tableau was used to visualize team performance across categories and identify patterns in player satisfaction.

## Key Insights
- Teams with higher **facility grades** consistently rank higher overall.
- Strong **nutrition programs** show a noticeable correlation with player satisfaction.
- Some teams score well in coaching but poorly in travel and facilities, highlighting operational gaps in player support.

## Visualization
A Tableau dashboard was created to compare team rankings and category performance.

## Future Improvements
Future analysis could explore correlations between team spending, facilities investment, and player satisfaction scores. Additional seasons of NFLPA report card data could also be incorporated to analyze trends over time.
